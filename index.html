THE HORIZON PROBLEM - PROGRESSIVE ROCK BAND



About:

The Horizon Problem is a progressive rock band from New York City formed in in 2014. Their sound is often described as Prog Alternative as they are
heavily influenced by bands like Soundgarden, Rush and Cooheed and Cambria. They have been giging around NYC's famed Lower East Side for years even selling out the famous Arlenes grocery in 2016. The
long term goals of The Horizon Problem will be to have a full length album (this is in the works now)
and to eventually go on tour with other bands in the Progressive Rock genre.
