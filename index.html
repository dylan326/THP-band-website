THE BAND

Progressive Rock!

Formed in 2014 The Horizon Problem is a New York City Progressive Rock band focused on adding hooks to complex odd time signature music. Six song EP is currently in the works, expecting release in mid-2018. Our influences include Animals as Leaders, CHON, Stone Temple Pilots and Coheed and Cambria.
